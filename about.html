This site was made by Sam Kupfer. Feel free to email me at shkupfer@gwu.edu with any comments or suggestions.

All data used on this site was pulled from the World Bank Open Data website. It consists of education, healthcare, and military expenditures data for all G20 countries (not including the European Union) from 2009 to 2015. Some data is missing from the World Bank's site, so it is also missing from the graphics on this site.
